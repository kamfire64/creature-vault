The battle system works, but it needs better balance, longer fights, and better creature art.

PART 1 — Make battles longer and more strategic
Current battles end too quickly. Rebalance combat so a normal 3v3 battle lasts around 8–15 turns.

Change damage formula:
- Current formula is too high.
- New formula:
  damage = max(4, round((attack * 0.65) - (defense * 0.25)))
  then apply type multiplier.
- Type advantage should be 1.2x instead of 1.25x.
- Type disadvantage should be 0.85x instead of 0.8x.
- Critical hits should be rare: 8% chance, 1.35x damage.

Increase effective HP:
- Increase all creature HP by about 35–50%.
- Do not increase attack as much.
- Tanks should feel tanky, speedsters should not instantly delete enemies.

Add energy system:
- Every active creature has Energy from 0 to 100.
- Basic Attack gives +25 energy.
- Defend gives +35 energy.
- Skill costs 60 energy instead of using only cooldown.
- Ultimate costs 100 energy and is only available for Epic/Legendary creatures.
- This makes players plan turns instead of spamming skills.

Add meaningful action choices:
Attack:
- medium damage, builds energy.

Defend:
- reduces incoming damage by 50%.
- clears one negative status effect if the creature has one.
- builds extra energy.

Skill:
- costs 60 energy.
- stronger than attack.
- applies type effect.
- cannot be used unless enough energy.

Ultimate:
- Epic and Legendary only.
- costs 100 energy.
- big effect but not instant win.
- add strong animation.

Swap:
- Swap should consume the turn, but the incoming creature gets 20 energy.
- AI should swap less often so battles do not feel annoying.

Status effect balance:
- Burn should deal 5% max HP per turn for 3 turns.
- Regen should heal 6% max HP per turn for 3 turns.
- Stun should only last 1 turn and have a 35% application chance, not guaranteed.
- Shield should reduce incoming damage by 35%, not 50%, unless defending.

Enemy AI improvements:
- Enemy should not always make perfect choices.
- Add simple difficulty levels:
  Easy: random-ish choices
  Normal: decent choices
  Hard: smarter choices
- Default to Normal.
- Normal AI should sometimes attack even if skill is available.

Rewards:
- Give XP based on battle length and creatures defeated.
- Give bonus coins for winning with all 3 creatures alive.

Important:

- Create original CSS/SVG pixel-art creatures with more details.

Improve card display:

- Background color should match type.

- Legendary should have a more dramatic idle effect.
